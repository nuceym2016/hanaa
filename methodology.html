Order of Estate Claims

Before any assets are distributed among heirs, the engine mandates the execution of claims in this chronological order:

  1. Immediate estate clearance costs (Funeral/Burial adjustments).
  2. Settlement of all verified outstanding liabilities and creditor debts.
  3. Execution of valid wills or testaments (restricted to a maximum of 1/3 of the net remaining estate).
  4. Distribution of the absolute residue among the surviving legal heirs.
